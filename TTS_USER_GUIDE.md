# 🎤 Text-to-Speech Feature - User Guide

## What is Text-to-Speech?

The Text-to-Speech (TTS) feature allows you to **listen to story chapters** being read aloud by your browser's built-in voice synthesis. Perfect for:
- 👁️ Reducing eye strain during long reading sessions
- 🚗 Listening while commuting or doing other tasks
- 📚 Improving comprehension through audio
- ♿ Accessibility for visually impaired users

---

## How to Use

### Starting Playback

1. **Navigate to any story chapter** you want to listen to
2. **Look for the microphone icon (🎤)** in the reader toolbar at the top
3. **Click the microphone button** to start reading

```
Reader Toolbar:
[A-] [A] [A+] │ [≡] [Tl] [◼] [🎤]
                              ↑
                         Click here!
```

### Controlling Playback

| Action | What Happens | Icon |
|--------|--------------|------|
| **First Click** | Starts reading from beginning | 🎤 → ⏸️ |
| **Second Click** | Pauses reading | ⏸️ → ▶️ |
| **Third Click** | Resumes reading | ▶️ → ⏸️ |

### Visual Indicators

The button changes color to show what's happening:

- **Gray** 🎤 = Ready to start (idle)
- **Green** ⏸️ = Currently reading (playing)
- **Orange** ▶️ = Paused (can resume)

### Automatic Stops

The reading will **automatically stop** when you:
- ✅ Reach the end of the chapter
- ✅ Click "Previous" or "Next" chapter
- ✅ Select a different chapter from the dropdown
- ✅ Navigate away from the page
- ✅ Close the browser tab

### Dark Mode

The TTS button works seamlessly with dark mode! The colors adjust automatically when you toggle dark mode.

## Browser Compatibility

### ✅ Fully Supported
- **Google Chrome** (version 33+)
- **Microsoft Edge** (version 14+)
- **Safari** (version 7+)
- **Opera** (version 21+)

### ⚠️ Partially Supported
- **Firefox** (version 49+) - Limited voice options

### ❌ Not Supported
- **Internet Explorer 11** - Feature will be hidden

> **Note:** If you don't see the microphone button, your browser doesn't support this feature. Try updating your browser or using a different one.

## Tips & Tricks

### 🎯 Best Practices

1. **Start with shorter chapters** to test the feature
2. **Use headphones** for better audio quality
3. **Adjust your device volume** before starting
4. **Pause before navigating** to avoid abrupt stops

### 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Button doesn't appear
- **Solution:** Update your browser or try Chrome/Edge

**Problem:** No sound when clicking
- **Solution:** Check your device volume and unmute your browser tab

**Problem:** Voice sounds robotic
- **Solution:** This is normal for browser TTS - voice quality depends on your operating system

**Problem:** Reading stops unexpectedly
- **Solution:** Make sure your tab stays active and your device doesn't go to sleep

**Problem:** Can't pause or resume
- **Solution:** Refresh the page and try again

## Keyboard Navigation

You can use your keyboard to navigate to the TTS button:

1. Press **Tab** repeatedly to cycle through page elements
2. When the microphone button is highlighted, press **Enter** or **Space**

## Privacy & Security

### 🔒 Your Privacy is Protected

- ✅ **No data is sent to external servers**
- ✅ **No recording or storage of audio**
- ✅ **No tracking or analytics**
- ✅ **100% browser-based processing**

The TTS feature uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis engine. Everything happens locally on your device.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change the voice?
Currently, the feature uses your browser's default voice. Future updates may include voice selection.

### Can I adjust the reading speed?
Not yet, but this feature may be added in future updates. The current speed is optimized for comfortable listening.

### Does it work on mobile?
Yes! The feature works on mobile browsers that support the Web Speech API (Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android).

### Will it drain my battery?
TTS uses minimal resources. Battery impact is similar to playing audio in your browser.

### Can I use it offline?
Yes! Since it's browser-based, it works offline as long as the page is loaded.

### Does it work with all languages?
The feature is optimized for English content. Other languages may work depending on your browser's voice support.
